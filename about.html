About us

Creation

Watchlist was created as a student project by me, Keno. When considering what to develop, I came up with this idea because I thought it would be useful for myself as well.

Idea behind Watchlist

Watchlist is a website designed to help users keep track of the shows and movies they want to watch , so they never forget them or keep track of the ones they watched. Users can create multiple personalized watchlists, of movies ,shows they want to watch or keep track of movies ,shows they watched in their stash. Where they can also add rating or comments. The site also features login and registration options, allowing users to securely manage their lists and access them anytime.
